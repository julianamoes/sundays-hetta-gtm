How to use this calendar

Brand toggle

Use the Both Sundays Hetta buttons in the toolbar to switch your view.

  • Both — shows Sundays and Hetta dates side by side in the same table, colour-coded so you can compare at a glance.
  • Sundays or Hetta — focuses on one brand only, with a single date column.

Drops

The calendar is organised into three seasonal drops — Sept Drop, Jan Drop, and March Drop. Each drop has its own full GTM timeline for both brands. Use the tabs at the top to switch between them.

Anchor date — Season Launch

Every date in the calendar is calculated relative to the Season Launch step for that drop. The “Weeks to Launch” column shows how many weeks before (negative) or after (positive) the launch each step falls. If the launch date changes, all other dates shift accordingly.

Date colours

  • Red date — this step is overdue (date has passed).
  • Green date — this step is coming up this week.

Filters & search

  • Search bar — type any keyword to filter steps by title, description, stage, or notes.
  • Type filter — filter by Checkpoint, Deadline, or Step.
  • Stage filter — filter by process stage (e.g. PSR, CSR).
  • Switch between List and Timeline views using the toggle on the right.

Calendar view

Shows all steps plotted on a monthly calendar. Use the brand toggle and drop checkboxes in the toolbar to control what’s displayed. Click any chip on the calendar to see the step details.

Overlap view

Combines all drops into one chronological timeline so you can see where Jan, March, and Sept work overlaps. Useful for spotting busy periods across the full year.

Steps tab

This is the core master list of all GTM process steps. It shows the standard week offsets for both Sundays and Hetta, independent of any specific drop. Use this as the process reference.
